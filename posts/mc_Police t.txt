News Heading : Police to expand drug investigation involving Malayalam actors
Description : On Wednesday, the police announced plans to widen their investigation into the suspected drug abuse case, now set to involve prominent Malayalam film actors. This development follows the recent arrest of a notorious gangster and his associates at a luxury hotel, where new leads reportedly surfaced, linking the actors to the case.
