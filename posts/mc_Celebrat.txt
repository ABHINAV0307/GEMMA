News Heading : Celebrating Kader Khan: A timeless icon of Indian cinema on his 87th birth anniversary
Description : Kader Khan did many things starting from acting, screenwriting, and producing films but he was recognised for his collaborations with actors such as Amitabh Bachchan, Rajesh Khanna, Jeetendra, Anil Kapoor, and especially Govinda. The late actor has secured a place for himself in the hearts of cinema lovers. He died in December 2018 due to Progressive Supranuclear Palsy, a degenerative disease that causes loss of balance, difficulty in walking and dementia.
