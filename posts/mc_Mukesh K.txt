News Heading : Mukesh Khanna describes Ranveer Singh as 'too playful and mischievous' for Shaktimaan's role
Description : Veteran actor Mukesh Khanna, renowned for his iconic portrayal of Shaktimaan on Indian television, has voiced his concerns regarding the casting of Ranveer Singh in the upcoming film adaptation of the show. Khanna expressed doubts about Singh being the right choice for the role, highlighting his reservations about the casting decision.
