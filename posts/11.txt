News Heading : Shah Rukh Khan wins best actor for Jawan at IIFA Awards 2024, says "It’s good to be back"
Description : Superstar Shah Rukh Khan won the best actor award at the IIFA award night on Saturday, September 28, for his spectacular performance in Jawan. He revealed that he and his family faced a "tough time" during the development of Jawan. However, he expressed his joy in finishing the year on a high note after winning the Best Actor award for the action blockbuster at the IIFA Awards 2024 ceremony.
