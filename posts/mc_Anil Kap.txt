News Heading : Anil Kapoor turns down Rs 10 Crore worth pan masala endorsement
Description : Anil Kapoor recently made headlines for turning down a massive Rs 10 crore endorsement deal from a well-known pan masala brand, citing ethical concerns. The veteran actor, known for his integrity and commitment to social responsibility, made it clear that endorsing such products contradicts his values as both an entertainer and a public figure.
