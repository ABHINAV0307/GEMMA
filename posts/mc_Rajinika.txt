News Heading : Rajinikanth's Vettaiyan advance ticket booking is more than GOAT, Indian 2 and Raayan: Report
Description : Reports indicate that bookings for Vettaiyan have exceeded those of Thalapathy Vijay’s The Greatest of All Time (GOAT) by an impressive 54%. To date, more than 1.40 lakh tickets have been sold on BookMyShow, a testament to Rajinikanth's enduring popularity and the film’s buzz among fans.
