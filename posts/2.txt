News Heading : Moodeng memecoin user invests Rs 1 lakh to make over Rs 100 crore in 17 days
Description : Moodeng coin is named after Moo Deng -- a two-month-old pint-sized pygmy hippo who has gained massive internet popularity because of her cheeky expressions and playful frolicking. The memecoin is the latest product of a niche online culture that also birthed Dogecoin and Shiba Inu.
