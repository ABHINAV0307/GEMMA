News Heading : Kabir Khan, Imtiaz Ali, Rima Das, and Onir's My Melbourne set for screening at MAMI
Description : After its world premiere at the 2024 Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM), My Melbourne, a distinctive anthology directed by four renowned Indian filmmakers—Imtiaz Ali, Kabir Khan, Rima Das, and Onir—is gearing up for its India premiere at the 2024 Mumbai Film Festival (MAMI).
