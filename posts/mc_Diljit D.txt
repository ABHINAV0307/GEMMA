News Heading : Diljit Dosanjh’s Dil-Luminati India tour tickets for Delhi and Jaipur shows sold out in just 6 minutes again, fans express disappointment
Description : Diljit Dosanjh has thrilled his fans by adding two more shows in Jaipur and Delhi to his much-anticipated Dil-Luminati Tour 2024 which is set to begin in India on October 26, 2024. He had earlier announced that tickets for these newly scheduled performances will be available starting today, October 9, at 2 PM through Zomato. However, the tickets were sold out with 6 minutes as soon as the tickets were available.
