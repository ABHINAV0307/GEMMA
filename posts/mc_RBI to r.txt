News Heading : RBI to review feedbacks on MFI guidelines when needed, says deputy governor Rao
Description : Some NBFC-MFIs have been requesting the regulator to revise the criteria relating to annual household income limit for giving microloans in view of their growing needs of individual borrowers. They want this limit upped from the existing Rs 3 lakh to about Rs 5 lakh.
