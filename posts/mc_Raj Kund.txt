News Heading : Raj Kundra takes legal action over defamatory media coverage over illegal immigration
Description : Raj Kundra, a businessman and public figure, has taken legal action following defamatory media coverage that falsely linked him to an illegal immigration case. The case involves claims made in a news article and a YouTube video, which connected Raj Kundra to Riya Barade, who is involved in a criminal case.
