News Heading : 12th fail director Vidhu Vinod Chopra reveals his father's reaction when he told him he wants to be a director
Description : On his recent visit to the 14th Indian Film Festival that was held on 12 and 13th October. Vidhu Vinod Chopra revealed that his father slapped him when he said he wanted to make films. His father cautioned him that pursuing a career in Mumbai would not lead to financial stability. Chopra shared that he had to work his way to FTII, where he learnt the craft.
