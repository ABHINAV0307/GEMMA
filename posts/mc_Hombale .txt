News Heading : Hombale films celebrates historic wins at the 70th National Film Awards
Description : This year’s National Film Awards saw Hombale Films making a significant impact with multiple wins. Rishab Shetty received the National Award for Best Actor for his role in Kantara, while the film itself clinched the award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment.
