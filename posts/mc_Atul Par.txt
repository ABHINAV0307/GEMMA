News Heading : Atul Parchure passes away, politician Raj Thackeray visits the actor's home to pay his last respects
Description : Atul Parchure, a well-known actor in both the Marathi and Hindi film industries, passed away on October 14 at the age of 57. His death has left a deep void in the hearts of his fans and the entertainment community. Atul had been fighting cancer for several years, and reports indicate that he experienced a relapse before his passing.
